# CoNest

![CoNest Logo](logo.png)

## Product Vision

CoNest is a community-oriented platform that connects students seeking affordable and stable housing with elderly homeowners who have available living space.

Unlike traditional rental platforms, CoNest focuses on structured intergenerational co-living by introducing compatibility-based matching, clearly defined expectations, and a structured trial period to reduce risk for both parties. 

Through verified profiles, transparent reviews, and accountability mechanisms, the platform ensures trust, safety, and long-term stability in shared living arrangements.

By transforming unused residential space into meaningful shared living opportunities, CoNest reduces student housing insecurity while addressing loneliness among elderly individuals.
